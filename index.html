About Farah

Executive. Innovator.
Community Builder.

Farah Ali is a technology executive, keynote speaker, entrepreneur, and non-profit founder with a career spanning the world's most influential technology companies. Her journey from a new college graduate at Microsoft to Vice President of Engineering at Electronic Arts reflects a relentless pursuit of impact at scale.

At EA, Farah led engineering for the platform powering 500 million global players, growing her team from a handful of engineers to more than 100 people. She has since expanded her focus to AI Transformation and Technology Strategy — helping organizations define where and how emerging technologies reshape their future.

Beyond the corporate world, Farah co-founded Pakistani Women in Computing (PWiC), a global community connecting women in STEM, and One Good Act, a non-profit empowering communities living below the poverty line. She is an angel investor, startup advisor, and passionate advocate for representation in tech.
